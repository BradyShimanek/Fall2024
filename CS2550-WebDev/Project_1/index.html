My Personal Interests

I like playing music. Don't get it twisted--just because I like playing it, doesn't mean that I am good at playing it. I mainly just like the way it sounds when I learn a few chords and make up some songs on my own. My main instruments are the piano, guitar, and electric guitar.

I also like sports. My favorite sports are Basketball and Football. My favorite NBA team is the Utah Jazz of course. My favorite NFL team is the Dolphins. I used to live there for a couple years, and I never really paid attention to the NFL until I decided one day that it was fun to have a team that I can be connected to. My favorite college football teams are the U of U and BYU. Always been a BYU fan, but continually disappointed in them haha!

Another thing about me is that I like fishing. Mainly in the ocean, but seeing that I live in the great landlocked state of Utah, fishing in the ocean is pretty few and far between. I don't mind fishing in the lakes around here, but I personally don't have a boat or anything to get me out in the middle of the body of water to get the best fish. The great thing about ocean fishing is you can fish from a pier, or really anywhere and catch some of the coolest fish ever. And yes, I have caught a couple sharks.
